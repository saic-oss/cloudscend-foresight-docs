Introduction

The CloudScend Foresight product will save you time and money by analyzing your application and providing detailed reports that will give you useful information regarding the health of your source code, potential security issues associated with your application, and effort needed to upgrade and modernize your application to run in the cloud.

Using the CloudScend Foresight product, you will simply onboard your application, answer a few questions about your application, and upload your source code and you will soon have 5 detailed reports giving you the information you need to make informed decisions on cost and effort to modernize your application to run in the cloud. The CloudScend Foresight product has integrated best-in-class COTS and open source analysis tools to enable informed decision-making with the most up to date and accurate information available regarding your application's code health, quality and cloud readiness. And with continuous assessment capabilities, you can see all of this critical data over time as your code base changes and new versions are uploaded to CloudScend Foresight, either via API calls from your CI/CD pipeline or manual uploading processes.

The purpose of this guide is to help get you up and running with CloudScend Foresight today. This users guide will provide you with step by step instructions to set up your account, onboard your application, handle your configurations and analyze your CloudScend Foresight provided reports.
